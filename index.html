About Us

About Perry (Founder)

I've always been passionate about hair and when I started my career in hairdressing I knew that one day I wanted to create an atmosphere with a fun and happy vibe where our clients can feel comfortable, relaxed and confident and our hairdressers have a passion to come to work, be creative and be part of our hairdressing family that will benefit from the success of the salons growth. I appreciate how hard hairdressers work to support the industry and so recognition and reward is an integral part of the Lady K culture.

Two years on and surrounded by an amazing team of passionate hairdressers, who I like to call my extended family, my vision still remains the same.

Come experience a home away from home!
